Step 1: Visit the login website of the Countribution Management system

Step 2: Enter your username and password

Step 3: Click the Sign In button.

Note:

Remember me: Check this box if you want your computer to store your sign-in information for the next time you sign in.

If you forgot your password, click the Forgot password? to reset your password.

If you don't have an account, click the Sign up link to create a new account.

Step 1: Visit the registration website of the countribution management system

Step 2: Fill in the corresponding fields including:

Full name: Enter your full name.

Username: Enter the username you want to use.

Password: Enter the password you want to use.

Confirm password: Re-enter your password to confirm.

Email: Enter your email address.

Step 3: Choose your faculty and role.

Step 4: Choose a profile picture for your account (optional).

Step 5: Click the Register button. After activating your account, you can log in using your username and password.
